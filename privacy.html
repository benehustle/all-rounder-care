Privacy policy for NDIS participants in Perth

How All Rounder Care collects, uses, and stores the details you share when you enquire about disability support across Western Australia.

Who we are

All Rounder Care is a Western Australian provider of NDIS disability support operating from 4 Lilac Pass, Bertram WA 6167. For privacy questions, email kaileb.j.werner@gmail.com or phone 0451 465 518.

What this website collects

The contact form on contact.html asks for your name, email address, phone number, selected service, and message. Submitting the form currently shows an on-screen confirmation only; messages are not emailed automatically by this static website until a backend is connected. You should treat the form as a draft enquiry and follow up by phone if you need an urgent reply.

Phone and email enquiries

When you call or email, we record the details needed to prepare a quote or roster supports, such as your name, address, NDIS plan number (if you choose to share it), and health information relevant to safe support delivery. We store these details in line with NDIS practice requirements and Australian privacy law.

How we use information

We use participant and family information to schedule workers, meet reporting obligations, respond to incidents, and improve service quality. We do not sell personal information. We only share details with third parties when you ask us to — for example, your support coordinator — or when the law requires it.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not load analytics scripts or advertising trackers. Session storage may be used in your browser to remember that you dismissed the mobile call bar.

Access and correction

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you or ask us to correct inaccurate records. We will respond within a reasonable period and may need to verify your identity before releasing sensitive health information.

Complaints

If you believe we have mishandled your information, contact us first using the details above. You may also complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.
