How we work alongside you

All Rounder Care is a local, family-owned NDIS support provider based in Kwinana, Western Australia. We bring over ten years of combined experience in the disability sector, and we understand that no two participants are the same — which is why every plan stays flexible, tailored, and built around the individual.

We work closely with participants and their support networks to deliver meaningful, respectful support focused on dignity, trust, and genuine connection. Whether you need help with daily living, getting out into the community, or building long-term independence, we aim to be more than just a service — we want to be a team that truly cares.

From our base near Bertram, we travel across Perth — South of the River, keeping communication clear, paperwork tidy, and expectations realistic. When your goals shift, we adjust the roster with you rather than rushing a one-size-fits-all package.

If you would like to talk through funding, schedules, or a new goal on your plan, call us for a free quote and a straightforward conversation about what comes next.

What guides our visits

Three principles shape how we show up at your door, in your car, or beside you in the community.

Respect first

We follow your cues on personal space, routines, and how you like tasks done — especially in personal care and shared living spaces.

Transparent planning

We explain how supports align with your NDIS plan in everyday language so families can make informed decisions without wading through jargon.

Reliable follow-through

When we agree to a time or a task, we treat it as a commitment. If something changes, we let you know early and organise a practical backup.
