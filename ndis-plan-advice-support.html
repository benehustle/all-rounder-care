Plain-language plan guidance

NDIS plans bundle several funding lines, each with rules about what can be purchased. All Rounder Care helps participants and families across Perth — South of the River translate plan language into weekly supports — linking core, capacity building, and capital budgets to the services we deliver or to other local providers you already trust.

We are not financial advisers, but we can sit with you before planning meetings, list questions for your support coordinator, and show how rostered hours consume funding over a plan cycle.

What is involved

Sessions may include reviewing service agreements, comparing quote formats, preparing a simple calendar of supports, and checking that travel or non-face-to-face claims fit NDIS price guide rules current at the time. We highlight when a specialist plan manager or local area coordinator should answer a question instead of us.

If your goals mention employment, education, or health, we signpost Western Australian services that commonly pair with NDIS funding so you can follow up directly.

What to expect when we meet

We bring exemplar schedules, explain how cancellations might affect budgets, and store notes you approve. Meetings can happen at home, by phone, or beside you at an NDIA or partner office if you want moral support — though official decisions always sit with you and the Agency.

Afterwards we email a short summary of agreed actions so everyone in your network hears the same version.

Three clear benefits

  • Less guesswork when lining up supports with remaining funds.
  • Earlier spotting of gaps before crisis meetings.
  • Consistent messaging between family, provider, and coordinator.

Questions families often ask

Can you help me challenge a planning decision from the NDIA?

We can help you organise paperwork and timelines, but formal reviews and appeals follow NDIA processes and often need a support coordinator or advocate. We will point you to those pathways clearly.

How do I know if a support fits the NDIS price guide?

We check the support catalogue item, region, and time of day against the guide published by the NDIA for the relevant financial year. If something is unclear, we pause invoicing until your plan manager or the NDIA confirms eligibility.

Do you charge differently for phone-based plan advice?

Rates follow the NDIS price guide for the relevant support item, including any permitted non-face-to-face portion. We quote before the work starts so you can compare with other providers.
